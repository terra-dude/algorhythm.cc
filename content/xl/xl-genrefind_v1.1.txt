DESCRIPTION
-----------
This script is made for those who have psxc-imdb and use the .imdb feature.
It searches a certain section for movies with a specific genre/rating.
Usage: !grfind <section> <genre> <imdbrating (x.x or x.x-x.x)> [min votes] [max hits] [Opening year+]

INSTALLATION
------------
* Extract the file ( gzip -dc xl-genrefind_v1.1.tar.gz | tar -xof- ) 
* Edit and copy xl-genrefind.sh to /glftpd/bin and chmod a+x
* Copy xl-genrefind.tcl to your eggdrop script dir and add it to your eggdrop.conf
