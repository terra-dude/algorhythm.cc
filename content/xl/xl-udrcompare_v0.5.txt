DESCRIPTION
-----------
Lists the top 5 UL/DL ratios.
Available commands are: !altopratio, !mntopratio, !wktopratio, !daytopratio [Upload Limit].

INSTALLATION
------------
* Extract the file ( gzip -dc xl-udrcompare_v0.5.tar.gz | tar -xof- )
* Edit and copy xl-udrcompare.sh to /glftpd/bin and chmod a+x
* Copy xl-udrcompare.tcl to your eggdrop script dir and add it to your eggdrop.conf
