Release Traffic - Pentagon
Shell script with a Tcl frontend.

Load the .tcl in your bot, and take a look at the example config.
If you don't like the output, then edit the putquick line at the end.

Example:
<you> !traffic A.Movie.DVDR-RELEASE
<bot> (SiTE) A.Movie.DVDR-RELEASE Was uploaded by 3 users from 1 group (3954 MB) and leeched by 3 users from 2 groups (715 MB)
