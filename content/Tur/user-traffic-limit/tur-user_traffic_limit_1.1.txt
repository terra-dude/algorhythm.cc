Tur-User_Traffic_Limit (GL1&2)

Description: A script that will monitor and prohibit users from downloading and/or 
uploading too much in a given duration (DAY/WEEK/MONTH).
Good for those with limited bandwidth who want to prevent a single user from eating
it all up.
