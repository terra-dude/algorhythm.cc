Birthday (GL1&2)

Description: A birthday script. Users can add themselfs to the db with when they 
have their birthday and on that day they will be given a present automatically =)
The present may consist of leech for 24 hours and/or credits. A percentage can be 
set to be given leech and a min-max number for how much credits. Has a few more 
gadgets but thats basically it.
Ofcourse, it can also announce in the chan to congratulate the user and tell him 
what he got.
