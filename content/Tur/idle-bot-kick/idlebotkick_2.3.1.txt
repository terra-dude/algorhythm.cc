IdleBotKick (GL1&2)

Description: Can do two things. If execute from irc, with !kick and no arguments, 
it will kick all users that are idle. It can also take one username as an argument,
and will in that case kick that user. For ops only per default but that can be 
changed in the tcl (change bind to -|- or so).
