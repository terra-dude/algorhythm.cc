Tur-ListIdle (GL1&2)

Description: A script to display all users and when they last logged on. Ment for 
glftpd (not irc).
Can also be configured so that someone can only see the users in the group in which
they are gadmin, or with glftpd 2.+, all the groups in which they are gadmins.
