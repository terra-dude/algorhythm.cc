BW (GL1&2)

Description: A script for showing total bandwidth on site.
Uses tur-ftpwho (3.0+) available from this website aswell.
Dirs can be excluded for noshow and the output can be easely changed. 
Can also run, say, 3 times with 2 seconds in between and show the average bandwidth
over those 3 runs.
