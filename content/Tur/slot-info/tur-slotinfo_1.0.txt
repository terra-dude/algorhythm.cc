Tur-SlotInfo (GL1&2)

Description: A script to display, in irc and/or shell, the gadmin slots information
on a group.
Output might look something like

!slotinfo SiTEOPS
Total users for group SiTEOPS: 4 - No gadmins found.

!slotinfo Osten
Total users for group Osten: 8 - # of gadmins: 1 (OstChefen)
Total slots: 8 (8 used, 0 free).
Leech slots: 6 (4 used, 2 free).
