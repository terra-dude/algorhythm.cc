### Installation ###
Edit search.tcl and change it to fit your needs.
Do the same with search.sh.
Copy the search.tcl to your eggdrop's script directory.
Add "source scripts/search.tcl" to your eggdrop config file.
If you are going to use dcc send result you will need 
have the transfer module loaded in your eggdrop.conf.. 
Copy search.sh to your glftpd bin directory and chmod a+x.
make the dir where you will save the result file 777.
Then it's done. If your bot was running rehash it.
